## Color Scheme

**Primary Colors:**
- Primary: Material Blue
- Secondary: Grey shades
- Success: Green
- Error: Red
- Background: White/Light Grey

**Text Colors:**
- Primary Text: Dark Grey/Black
- Secondary Text: Medium Grey
- Disabled: Light Grey

**Badge Colors:**
- EXPIRED: Red background
- ACTIVE: Green background
- STUDENT: Blue badge
- Owner: Orange badge

## Typography

- **Headlines:** Large bold text (24-32px)
- **Body:** Regular text (14-16px)
- **Small:** Secondary text (12-14px)
- **Labels:** Bold small text (12px)
